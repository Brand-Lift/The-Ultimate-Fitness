The Muscle Lab Gym isn't just a place to work out — it's a laboratory for human potential. The Muscle Lab Gym is open Monday to Saturday from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and on Sundays from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We offer both early morning and late evening slots to accommodate all schedules — whether you're an early bird or a night owl, we've got a time slot that works for you.

Basic diet tips and nutritional guidance are included with all membership plans at no extra cost. For Pro and Elite members, our certified nutrition coaches create fully customized diet plans based on your specific body composition, fitness goals, and lifestyle.
